# Demo Runbook

Use this runbook for the final Phase 9 demo after automated gates pass.

## Six-Step Wow Demo

1. Open Fan App discovery and show the session disclosure plus ranked social cards.
2. Play Solar Sarah content and show contextual ad/receipt behavior.
3. Open Solar Sarah's channel, ask Archive Q&A, and show cited answers plus receipts.
4. Enter the clean-energy giveaway campaign and show campaign/reward receipt state.
5. Open Wallet and show supported-creators transparency.
6. Switch to Creator Studio, create an export, and review the portable bundle summary.

## Author-To-Consume Loop

1. Switch to Creator Studio.
2. Complete creator onboarding if needed.
3. Open publishing setup.
4. Generate the AI summary draft.
5. Publish the demo video.
6. Switch to Fan App.
7. Find and open the newly published content card.
8. Confirm playback opens and the fan can continue to support through Wallet.

## Reset Procedure

1. Open the shell demo menu.
2. Select Reset demo.
3. Confirm the app returns to Fan App.
4. Confirm discovery seed content and session disclosure are visible.

## Launch Loop

Use this loop for Phase 14 emulator validation before the later physical-phone sign-off.

1. Open Fan App discovery and switch to immersive discovery.
2. Return to dense discovery and open the creator invite.
3. Confirm the starter pack and verify the fan lands on a populated feed.
4. Open Solar Sarah playback and verify content/ad presentation.
5. Switch to Creator Studio and open Conversion to review aggregate launch yield.
6. Open one utility console: catalog import, ad policy, archive AI, or membership setup.
7. Open export/reset and confirm the seeded baseline can be restored.

## Validation Targets

- Flutter Android emulator: install, launch, complete all six steps.
- Physical Android phone: authoritative final sign-off moved to Phase 20; Phase 14 can run a preliminary phone smoke if hardware is available.
